ABOUT US

Welcome

Our mission is to share with you our passion for watersports. Our club is much more than just a sport, a culture or a lifestyle.
When you enter NRT Kiteboarding Club, your Life will change!
Whatever your needs, from taking lessons in kiting or Stand Up Paddle boarding, from total beginner to most advanced rider,
from buying the latest gear and equipment to organizing an afternoon activity with your mates,
we’re here to answer all your questions and give you an unforgettable and unique experience

EVENTS

We Provide Better

Wave Regatta 2022

Join us during the Wave Regatta 2022 which will take place on the 3rd, 4th, 5th and 6th of July! Whether you would like to participate to our Kite/SUP race or paddle around the sailboats and the festival, we're here for you!

SUP Summer Camps

After a very sucessful year in 2021 and a lot of happy faces, we are organising summer camps this summer again! Our qualified instructors will teach your little ones Stand Up Paddleboarding in the form of games and activities for 2hrs a day! Wetsuits, life jackets and booties (or old runners) are to be brought by each kid. We'll provide with boards, paddles and fun!

Battle For The Bay

We have always had the will to bring people together to celebrate out passion. Join us during the Battle For The Bay festival 6th, 7th and 8th of August! We are here in our little paradise waiting for you! It will be all about watersport, music and fun!

BBQ Sunday Chill

We will have barbecue and DJ's every Sunday during the summer 2022 and a lot of fun so, join us!
